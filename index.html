How Zone Vault Works?

How to Login/Register

To join, you must first be part of a private livestream group. Before the event, the admin will provide step-by-step instructions on how to register and log in. You will need to input your TG username and a unique access code provided by the admin. This ensures that only authorized participants can access the stream. Once your request is submitted, the admin will review and approve it through the admin panel.

How Login/Register Works

All login and registration data are stored securely in database. All emails, passwords, and login activity are recorded to create a unique login ID for each user.

  • Important: Do not use your Gmail password — create a new, unique password for Zone Vault.
  • If you forget your password, contact zonevaultph@gmail.com for a reset.
  • Your login history is visible to the admin to maintain a safe and organized platform.

How Videos Work

We use HLJS + Plyr Player to ensure smooth playback and high-quality streaming

Additional Guidelines & Tips

  • Use a modern browser for the best viewing experience.
  • Ensure your internet connection is stable to avoid buffering.
  • Keep your login credentials confidential—sharing them may result in account suspension.
  • Contact the admin via email for support or technical issues.
